### Verdict: ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê (Excellent)
This strategy is highly effective because it targets **Cognitive Dissonance**.
*   **The Hook:** The user is currently living in confusion ("Why is this happening?").
*   **The Promise:** You aren't promising "Love" or "Happily ever after" (which feels fake to someone in pain). You are promising **Sense** (Logic/Clarity).
*   **The Psychology:** When someone feels crazy/gaslit, they crave *reality testing* more than affection. "Make it make sense" is the perfect CTA for the "Malice Detective" or "Anxious" attachment style.
