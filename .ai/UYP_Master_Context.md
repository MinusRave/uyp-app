## 3. THE USER AVATAR & PSYCHOLOGY
Our users are **Exhausted**. They are in a state of **Cognitive Dissonance**: "I love this person, but I feel unsafe/unheard/crazy."

### Core Dynamic: The "Chaser" vs. The "Runner"
Most of our users fall into this classic trap (Anxious-Avoidant trap):
*   **The Chaser (Anxious):**
    *   *Internal Monologue:* "If we don't fix this NOW, it means you don't care. Silence feels like abandonment."
    *   *Behavior:* Explodes, pursues, text-bombs, seeks reassurance.
    *   *Need:* "I need to know I matter."
*   **The Runner (Avoidant/Container):**
    *   *Internal Monologue:* "I can't do anything right. I need to get away to stop making it worse."
    *   *Behavior:* Shuts down, withdraws, goes silent, leaves the room.
    *   *Need:* "I need to know I am competent/autonomous."

**The Trap:** The Chaser gets loud -> The Runner shuts down -> The Chaser panics more -> The Runner retreats further.

## 5. CORE FRAMEWORK (THE SCIENCE)
The product is based on analyzing users across **5 Core Dimensions** using a **Quadrant System**.

### The 5 Dimensions
1.  **Silence & Distance:** How you react to space (Secure vs Abandonment Panic).
2.  **Conflict & Tension:** Tolerance for arguments (Urgent Resolver vs Avoider).
3.  **Intentional Attribution:** Do you assume they *meant* to hurt you? (Malice Detective vs Benefit of Doubt).
4.  **Reassurance Need:** How much verbal validation do you need? (Validation Hungry vs Self-Assured).
5.  **Repair & Closure:** Can you "let go" without a long talk? (Quick Eraser vs Lingering Resentment).

### The Quadrant System (The "Why")
We score every dimension on two axes:
1.  **Sensitivity (SL):** How much does this trigger your body? (Biological)
2.  **Perception of Malice (PM):** Do you think they did it on purpose? (Cognitive)

**Profiles:**
*   **Amplified Distress:** High Sensitivity + High Malice Perception (Max Pain).
*   **Detached Cynicism:** Low Sensitivity + High Malice Perception (Checked out).
*   **Self-Regulation:** High Sensitivity + Low Malice (It hurts, but I know you love me).
*   **Secure Flow:** Low Sensitivity + Low Malice (Easy going).
