# Ad Strategy: The "5-Dimensional Diagnostic" Campaign

**The Core Concept:**
Stop selling a "General Relationship Fix".
Start selling a **"Specific Symptom Diagnosis"**.
Users click because they feel a *specific* pain. The Landing Page then validates that pain and offers the full MRI.

## Angle 1: Physical Intimacy (The "Roommate" Trap)
*Targeting: "Roommates" Card on Landing Page.*

*   **Hook (Visual):** A couple sitting on a couch, phones out, miles apart emotionally.
*   **Headline:** "When did you become roommates?"
*   **Body Copy:**
    > "You function perfectly as a team. You pay the bills, feed the kids, and manage the house. But the spark? Gone. 
    > You aren't fighting. You're just... drifting.
    > 
    > It's not 'just a phase'. It's a specific biological loop called **The Intimacy Mismatch**.
    > 
    > Stop guessing why they pulled away. Run the diagnostic."
*   **CTA:** "Decode Your Intimacy Pattern (Free)"
