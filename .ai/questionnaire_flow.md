## Part 2: The Test
*Main assessment - User answers on a scale from "Strongly disagree" to "Strongly agree"*

### Dimension 1: Communication (Styles)
1.  When my partner is silent, I panic and think they are pulling away from me.
2.  During a fight, my partner tends to 'shut down' or walk away.
3.  I feel a strong, urgent need to resolve our arguments immediately.
4.  We often have the same exact argument over and over again.
5.  My partner avoids difficult conversations/conflict to keep the peace.
6.  I can handle unresolved tension without falling apart. (Reverse coded)
7.  During conflicts, I am often told that I am 'too sensitive,' 'crazy,' or remembering things wrong.

### Dimension 2: Emotional Safety (Bond/Trust)
8.  I barely feel 'heard' or understood by my partner.
9.  When my partner hurts me, I sometimes feel they did it on purpose.
10. I need frequent reassurance (words of affirmation) to feel safe.
11. I often replay our conversations in my head, analyzing what went wrong.
12. I generally assume my partner has my best interests at heart. (Reverse coded)
13. It takes us a long time to reconnect after a fight.
14. The relationship feels like a rollercoaster: my partner switches between intense affection and sudden coldness or disdain.

### Dimension 3: Physical Intimacy (Spark/Sex)
15. I feel we have become more like roommates than lovers.
16. I feel rejected as a person when my partner turns down sex.
17. I need to feel emotionally close *before* I can get physically close.
18. One of us wants sex significantly more than the other (mismatched desire).
19. I sometimes agree to sex just to keep the peace.
20. We touch affectionately (hugs, holding hands) often, even without sex. (Reverse coded)

### Dimension 4: Priorities & Fairness (Power)
21. I carry the 'mental load' (remembering schedules, planning) for our household.
22. I often feel like I am my partner's parent or manager.
23. Disagreements about money/spending make me feel unsafe.
24. I feel my partner doesn't truly understand how much I do for us.
25. I feel guilty when I rest, while my partner seems to relax easily.
26. We share decisions equally; neither of us dominates. (Reverse coded)

### Dimension 5: Future & Values (Vision)
27. I worry that we are growing in different directions.
28. I feel my partner prioritizes their parents/family over me.
29. We rarely 'dream' or purely have fun together anymore.
30. Our life goals (kids, career, lifestyle) feel very aligned. (Reverse coded)
