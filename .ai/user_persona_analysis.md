# User Persona & Motivation Analysis: The "Desperate Searcher"

**The User:** Typically a woman (65-70% of traffic) in her late 20s to 40s. Alternatively, a man who feels "unheard" despite trying.
**The State:** High Anxiety or High Frustration. They are not browsing; they are *searching for a cure*.

## 1. The Core Drivers (Why are they here?)
They didn't wake up and say, "I want to take a relationship test."
They woke up and said:
*   *"I can't do this anymore."*
*   *"Why does he/she always twist my words?"*
*   *"Am I the crazy one? Or is it them?"* (The gaslighting doubt).
*   *"Is this fixable, or should I leave?"* (The "Fork in the Road").

## 2. The Pain Points (The "Bleeding Neck")
They are not looking for "improvement." They are looking to stop the pain.
*   **The Communication Loop:** "We just had the SAME fight for the 100th time."
*   **The Loneliness:** "I am sitting next to them on the couch, but I feel completely alone."
*   **The Exclusion:** "They treat me like an employee/roommate, not a partner."
*   **The Confusion:** "One minute we are fine, the next minute it's World War 3. I don't know the trigger."

## 3. The Desired Outcome (What they WANT)
They don't want a "score." They want:
*   **Validation:** "See? I TOLD YOU I wasn't crazy!" (External authority).
*   **Translation:** "Oh, THAT is why they do that." (Clarity).
*   **Hope:** "Okay, this isn't broken. It's just a pattern we can fix."
*   **A Manual:** "Just tell me exactly what to say to stop the fight."

## 4. Why an Online Test? (The Value Prop)
Why not therapy? Why not a book?
*   **Therapy is expensive ($200/hr) and scary.** "I don't want to drag him there yet."
*   **Books take too long.** "I need an answer tonight."
*   **The Test is Low Risk:** It's private. It's objective. It's immediate.
*   **The "Science" Halo:** "It's not just my opinion; the *algorithm* says so." (Neutral Third Party).

## 5. The Benefits (What they are buying)
They are paying $29 for:
1.  **Immediate Relief:** "I finally understand what's happening."
2.  **The "Magic Words":** Scripts that actually work instantly.
3.  **Permission:** Permission to stay and fix it, OR permission to leave without guilt.
4.  **Status/Superiority:** (Subconscious) "I am the one trying/fixing this."

# Revised Landing Page Strategy: The "Empathy + Authority" Approach

**The Shift:**
*   **Less:** "Here is how our scoring algorithm works." (Too clinical).
*   **More:** "We know exactly how lonely you feel right now. Here is the map out."

## 1. The Hero Section
*   **Headline:** Must mirror the thought in their head at 2 AM.
    *   *Option A:* "Why do you feel so alone in this relationship?"
    *   *Option B:* "The fight isn't about the dishes. It's about 'Do I matter to you?'"
    *   *Option C:* "Stop the cycle before it breaks you."
*   **Subhead:** "See the invisible pattern driving your conflict in 10 minutes."
*   **CTA:** "Get My Answers Now" (Benefit-driven).

We must sell the **Result**, not the Report.
