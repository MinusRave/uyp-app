# User Flow Questions (Starting from /test)

This document lists every question and input asked of the user from the moment they land on the test page until they see the loading screen.

## 1. Onboarding Wizard
*Before the main test begins, users answer these profiling questions.*

### Step 1: Relationship Status
**Question:** "First, how would you describe your relationship right now?"
*   In Crisis ("We're on the brink of breaking up")
*   Unstable ("Frequent fights or emotional distance")
*   Stable but Stuck ("We're okay, but something is missing")
*   Recently Separated ("Trying to understand what happened")

### Step 2: History & Mechanics
**Question:** "How long have you been together?"
*   < 6 Months
*   6 Months - 2 Years
*   2 - 5 Years
*   5 - 10 Years
*   10+ Years

**Checkboxes:**
*   [ ] We live together
*   [ ] We have children

### Step 3: Conflict Dynamics
**Question:** "How often do you have conflicts?"
*   Daily
*   Weekly
*   Monthly
*   Rarely

**Question:** "When you fight, your partner typically:"
*   Withdraws / Goes silent
*   Escalates / Gets intense
*   Deflects / Blames you
*   Engages (Tries to resolve it calmly)

**Question:** "Do you successfully repair afterwards?"
*   Always - We make up quickly
*   Sometimes - It takes time
*   Rarely - We brush it under the rug
*   Never - Resentment builds up

## 2. Main Assessment (The "Relationship MRI")
*32 Questions answered on a 5-point scale (Strongly Disagree to Strongly Agree).*

### Communication
1. When my partner is silent, I panic and think they are pulling away from me.
2. During a fight, my partner tends to 'shut down' or walk away.
3. I feel a strong, urgent need to resolve our arguments immediately.
4. We often have the same exact argument over and over again.
5. My partner avoids difficult conversations/conflict to keep the peace.
6. I can handle unresolved tension without falling apart.

### Emotional Safety
7. I barely feel 'heard' or understood by my partner.
8. When my partner hurts me, I sometimes feel they did it on purpose.
9. I need frequent reassurance (words of affirmation) to feel safe.
10. I often replay our conversations in my head, analyzing what went wrong.
11. I generally assume my partner has my best interests at heart.
12. It takes us a long time to reconnect after a fight.

### Physical Intimacy
13. I feel we have become more like roommates than lovers.
14. I feel rejected as a person when my partner turns down sex.
15. I need to feel emotionally close *before* I can get physically close.
16. One of us wants sex significantly more than the other (mismatched desire).
17. I sometimes agree to sex just to keep the peace.
18. We touch affectionately (hugs, holding hands) often, even without sex.

### Priorities & Fairness
19. I carry the 'mental load' (remembering schedules, planning) for our household.
20. I often feel like I am my partner's parent or manager.
21. Disagreements about money/spending make me feel unsafe.
22. I feel my partner doesn't truly understand how much I do for us.
23. I feel guilty when I rest, while my partner seems to relax easily.
24. We share decisions equally; neither of us dominates.

### Future & Values
25. I worry that we are growing in different directions.
26. I feel my partner prioritizes their parents/family over me.
27. We rarely 'dream' or purely have fun together anymore.
28. Our life goals (kids, career, lifestyle) feel very aligned.
